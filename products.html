CP-500 Micronized Talc

500 Mesh, Brightness ≥95%

Industrial Grade • High Demand • Balanced Performance

Request a Quote

CP-500 is a high-purity micronized talc designed for plastics compounding, coatings, sealants, adhesives, and construction materials. It offers consistent particle size distribution, controlled oil absorption, and low moisture for stable processing and reliable batch-to-batch performance.

  • Best for: PP/TPO compounds, industrial coatings, joint compounds, sealants
  • Why buyers choose it: strong dispersion, balanced cost-performance, consistent PSD
  • Documentation: COA per batch, SDS available on request
