How It Works

  1. Application & Specification Review. We confirm required particle size distribution, brightness, oxide limits, and packaging to align with your process.
  2. Grade Lock & Documented Lot Control. Approved material is locked to a defined production lot with traceable chemical and physical properties.
  3. Batch-Level Quality Validation. Each lot undergoes documented review prior to release, with COA and supporting documentation prepared for shipment.
  4. Structured Canadian Delivery. Delivery is coordinated to the Port of Montréal or directly to your facility, with defined lead times and documentation compliance.
